Kobe Bryant

A Basketball Legend (Aug 23, 1978 - Jan 26, 2020)

Kobe Bryant alongside 5 NBA Championship Trophies

Timeline and Accomplishments

  • 1978: Born in Philadelphia
  • 1994: Pennsylvania Player of the Year Award
  • 1995: Several awards during senior year at Lower Merion High School
  • 1996: NBA draft No. 13 pick by Charlotte Hornet's then traded to Lakers
  • 1997: Slam Dunk Contest Champion
  • 2000-2002: Three-peat NBA Champion
  • 2006: Career-high 81-point game against the Toronto Raptors
  • 2009-2010: NBA Champion & Finals MVP
  • 2015: Retired after a 20-year career with the LA Lakers
  • 2020: Passed away in a tragic helicopter accident with his 13-year-old daughter, Gianna
